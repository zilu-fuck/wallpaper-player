## 更新内容

- 将播放器重构为更接近网页视频网站的嵌入式体验：16:9 黑色播放区域、视频居中显示、底部控制栏覆盖在画面内。
- 移除原先视频下方的大型参数面板和桌面菜单栏式体验，倍速、字幕、音轨、设置、清晰度改为小型悬浮菜单。
- 重做 mpv 嵌入逻辑，隐藏 mpv 原生 OSC，由应用自己的紧凑控制层直接覆盖在播放区域上。
- 优化控制栏交互：鼠标移动时显示，播放中静止后自动隐藏，暂停时中央显示播放按钮。
- 修复 mpv 初始窗口尺寸和网页全屏尺寸同步问题，减少刚打开时视频区域超出嵌入位置的情况。
- 修复播放队列问题：在分类中播放时，上一首/下一首/播放结束自动下一首会保持在当前分类队列，不再跳到其它分类。
- 调整底部传输按钮顺序为上一首、播放/暂停、下一首、重播。
- 优化音量控件悬停布局，避免光标放在音量区域时控件抖动。
- 调整方向键快捷键：短按左右方向键快退/快进，长按方向键临时倍速播放，松开后恢复原倍速。
- 增加播放状态保存，覆盖播放进度、音量、倍速、静音、字幕、音轨、字幕大小、循环和 A-B 循环设置。
- 拆分 Electron 主进程模块，收紧 IPC 命令白名单和视频路径校验，减少渲染进程可调用的危险面。

## Update Notes

- Rebuilt the player into a more web-video-like embedded experience with a 16:9 black playback stage, centered video, and bottom controls over the video.
- Removed the large below-video parameter panel and desktop-menu feel. Speed, subtitles, audio tracks, settings, and quality now use compact floating menus.
- Reworked mpv embedding so mpv's native OSC stays hidden while the app-managed control layer sits over the playback area.
- Improved control-bar interaction: controls appear on mouse movement, auto-hide during playback, and show a centered play button while paused.
- Fixed mpv initial sizing and web-fullscreen bounds syncing to reduce cases where the video host overflows the embedded area at startup.
- Fixed playlist navigation so previous/next/end-of-video playback stays inside the active category queue instead of jumping to another category.
- Changed transport button order to previous, play/pause, next, replay.
- Stabilized the volume hover layout to avoid jitter around the volume control.
- Changed arrow-key behavior: short left/right press seeks, long press temporarily speeds playback, and releasing restores the previous speed.
- Added playback-state persistence for position, volume, speed, mute, subtitles, audio track, subtitle size, loop, and A-B loop settings.
- Split the Electron main process into smaller modules and tightened IPC command whitelisting plus video path validation.
